# Parliament Watch Website Frontend

## Overview

Parliament Watch Uganda monitors and tracks the Ugandan Parliament on a regular basis, providing relevant data and expert insights. This frontend provides a comprehensive interface for accessing parliamentary data, news, blogs, trackers, multimedia content, and interactive features like a chatbot.

## Features

### Trackers
- **Members of Parliament (MPs) Tracker**: Browse and search MPs by party, district, or constituency with detailed profiles
- **Bills Tracker**: Track bills through their reading stages (1st, 2nd, 3rd reading, passed, assented, withdrawn)
- **Loans Tracker**: Monitor parliamentary loans by sector and source
- **Debt Tracker**: Track national debt information
- **Budget Tracker**: Access budget documents by financial year
- **Hansards Tracker**: Browse parliamentary proceedings and debates
- **Order Paper Tracker**: View parliamentary order papers
- **Parliament Performance Tracker**: Monitor parliamentary performance metrics

### News & Content
- **News & Updates**: Latest news articles with categories, pagination, and detailed article pages
- **Blogs**: Blog posts with filtering, search, and category organization

### Multimedia
- **X Spaces**: Archive of Twitter Spaces discussions
- **Podcasts**: Podcast library with episodes and categories
- **Gallery**: Photo gallery with categories and featured images

### Resources
- **Explainers**: Educational documents explaining parliamentary processes
- **Reports & Briefs**: Research reports and policy briefs
- **Partner Publications**: Publications from partner organizations
- **Statements**: Official statements and press releases
- **Committees**: Information about parliamentary committees

### Interactive Features
- **Chatbot**: AI-powered Q&A chatbot for parliamentary information (Alpha version)
  - Floating widget available on all pages
  - Full-page chatbot interface
  - Session-based conversations with chat history
  - Document source links for answers
- **Citizens' Voice**: Polls and public engagement features
- **Global Search**: Search across all content types (news, blogs, MPs, bills, resources, multimedia) with real-time suggestions

### Engagement
- **Contact Form**: Submit inquiries and feedback
- **Donation**: Support the organization through donations
- **About Us**: Learn about the organization's objectives and team members
